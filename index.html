Visit Chisinau

Enjoy culture and sports in this vibrant district close to the center of Chisinau.

Top three activities to do at Chisinau

Go for a piknic in the local park

The "Valea Morilor Park" is perfect for piknic, getting some tan, or just chilling out.

Attend a show or a concert

The National Theatre "Mihai Eminescu" is a great place to spend your night out.

Play sports at the "Valea Trandafirilor" beach

Play volleyball, football, basket ball or lift some weights.
